Wenhui Zhu (朱文辉)

I am a Ph.D. Candidate at the School of Computing and Augmented Intelligence (SCAI), Arizona State University, advised by Prof. Yalin Wang.

My research drives the full lifecycle of Generative AI, specializing in Efficient LLMs/VLMs/MLLMs, web-scale multimodal data curation, foundation model scaling, post-training alignment, and agentic reinforcement learning.

I design autonomous, long-horizon, Self-Evolving LLM/MLLMs agents capable of complex reasoning by stabilizing advanced post-training at scale and developing efficient training-free inference-time optimizations.

My findings are regularly published at top-tier venues including ICLR, EMNLP, ICML, ACL, CVPR, ECCV, ICCV, and COLM.
